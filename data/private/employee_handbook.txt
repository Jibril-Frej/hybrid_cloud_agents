ACME Corp — Employee Handbook (CONFIDENTIAL — INTERNAL USE ONLY)

Compensation and Benefits

All full-time employees are eligible for the ACME performance bonus program. The annual
bonus target is 15% of base salary for individual contributors and 25% for senior staff.
Bonus payouts are subject to individual performance rating and company financial results.

Remote Work Policy

Employees may work remotely up to three days per week with manager approval. Employees
handling data classified as RESTRICTED must work from a company-approved secure location.

Data Handling

Customer data must never be transmitted to third-party services without explicit legal
review. Internal project data classified CONFIDENTIAL must be stored on company-managed
infrastructure only. Any exception requires written approval from the Chief Security Officer.

Leave Policy

Employees accrue 20 days of paid time off (PTO) per year. PTO does not roll over beyond
5 days at calendar year end. Medical leave follows applicable local regulations.
