Artificial Intelligence Trends in 2024

Large language models (LLMs) have become the dominant paradigm in natural language processing.
Models such as GPT-4, Claude, and Gemini demonstrate strong general-purpose reasoning capabilities.
Open-source models like Llama and Mistral have significantly reduced the cost barrier for AI adoption.

Retrieval-Augmented Generation (RAG) combines vector search with generative models to ground
responses in factual documents. This technique reduces hallucinations and enables knowledge
to be updated without retraining the base model.

Key industry trends include: multimodal models that process text and images together,
smaller models optimized for on-device inference, and agentic systems that decompose
complex tasks into multi-step workflows.

The open-source AI ecosystem has matured with frameworks such as LangChain, LlamaIndex,
and LangGraph providing production-ready orchestration layers for AI applications.
